What is BUSD-T?

BUSD-T is a decentralized stablecoin pegged 1:1 with the US Dollar, built on the Binance Smart Chain. Designed for reliability, low-cost transfers, and global interoperability, BUSD-T empowers users to transact with confidence—without volatility, without borders.

Key Features

  • ✅ Pegged 1:1 to the US Dollar
  • ✅ Fast, secure transactions on BSC
  • ✅ Transparent and auditable contract
  • ✅ Designed for trading, saving, and settlement
  • ✅ Fully compatible with Trust Wallet, MetaMask, Binance Wallet, and more

Giving Back: Token with a Heart

BUSD-T isn't just a financial tool—it's a mission. We dedicate a portion of all proceeds from transactions and token operations to help underprivileged children receive access to quality education.

❤️ 5% of net earnings go directly to funding school supplies, scholarships, and tuition grants for children in need.
